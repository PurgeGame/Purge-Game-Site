How it Works

Players mint NFTs, which will be then be revealed at the start of the game. Each token has four traits, some of which are more rare than others. Ownership of a token will grant you access to the discord channels corresponding with its traits, where you can formulate strategies with fellow owners to win the game.

When a player chooses to purge a game piece, the NFT is destroyed and a ticket is created in the game's database. This ticket entitles the player to a payout at the end of the game if their token contained the trait which was the first to be exterminated.

Gamblers who just want to test their luck also have the option to "Mint and Purge" as many tokens as they would like. Players who choose this option will receive a ticket to the rewards generated exactly the same way as someone who minted and immediately purged their token. This method saves gas and is the simplest way to have a chance to win big.

Tickets generated in this fashion will be eligible for the MAP Jackpot. 10% of the prize pool generated by Mint and Purging will go towards this jackpot instead of the main prize. One ticket will be randomly selected near the start of the game to win the entire jackpot.

Players are also granted some $PURGED for each token they purge. This ERC20 token can be burned to mint Purge Game NFTs in future seasons.

The game is over as soon as any one trait has been completely purged. When a trait ceases to exist, everyone who purged a token with that trait will receive an equal payout, with a large bonus going to the player who completes the extermination.

The Endgame

As the number of remaining tokens dwindles, the stakes get higher. If you are the one who completes the extermination of a trait, you receive a massive bonus equal to 10% of the prize pool minus the MAP Jackpot.

Holders of NFTs with the same trait are thus incentivized to both cooperate and compete with one another. This is why, despite the uncomplicated mechanics, the game is strategically complex. Players are able to gain an edge through a diverse blend of both social and economic skills.

Purge Game staff will NEVER assist in any sort of dealmaking. All agreements between players are made at your own risk. Deceiving other players about your intentions is explicitly allowed.

Payouts

50% of revenue from primary sales will be placed into the prize pool and paid to the winners. These funds are üîílockedüîí into the contract and the ONLY way they can be released is by someone purging a token that eliminates a trait.

Players who have purged multiple winning game pieces receive an additive payout for each token purged. All winnings will be distributed in the same transaction as the purge which fulfils the victory condition. Prizes will be sent to the address which purged the token, no exceptions.

Because of how the contract is written, the game is unruggable. After I hit the reveal button, the entire dev team could die or the keys to the owner account could get stolen and the game would still work as intended. The prize money is safely locked by the game logic and guaranteed to be paid out in the manner described. The contract will be publicly available for review by nerds to confirm this fact before launch.

üí£The Bomb Tokenüí£

Purge Game is intended to be a fast-paced, exciting game that pays off quickly. There are some strategies and circumstances which could lead to a stalemate situation. To resolve this potential issue, if the game has not concluded by two weeks after reveal, a limited new token will enter the game. Starting at that time, once per day a single Bomb token will be airdropped into the account of a random player who has purged at any point.

The bomb token can be sold or consumed to purge ANY token remaining in the game. The potential prize for this purge will still go to the current owner of the token being bombed, but it will be removed from the game. This mechanic guarantees that the game will end in a reasonable timeframe.

The bomb could be a huge windfall to the random player who recieves it: if there is a trait with only two tokens remaining, either player could purchase the bomb, use it to destroy the other token, then purge theirs to win the grand prize.

My Inspiration

I had three main sources of inspiration for the idea behind Purge Game.

I've had this idea in my head for a while now, but the impetus that drove me to actually create it came from playing other NFT games. I enjoy them, and they satisfy my need for gambling and strategy, but basically every one of them suffers from the same absolutely infuriating problem. The devs can never follow through on their promises and they meet their own self-imposed deadlines approximately 0% of the time. Most of this occurs after they have collected a huge payday and half the time their game is immediately paused because it doesn't even work at all. Most of the other half, the game is buggy or exploitable by bad actors, as well as having too many on-chain gameplay actions that require paying a large gas tax.

One day, after a few particularly egregious examples of this occurring back-to-back-to-back, by ü§° devs who had each made millions of dollars from their NFT sales I had had enough. At that time, I resolved to make a game that was mechanically simple, while strategically complex, and would actually work, from start to finish. Purge Game has only one contract interaction, it costs a moderate amount of gas and it can only be done once per token.

I will not set deadlines until the product is ready to go. I will not release the game and take your money until a full mock game has been completed on testnet, from mint to payouts. I intend to build a brand that people can trust to follow through on its commitments. If Purge Game is a success, I‚Äôm sure there will be copy/paste ‚Äúdevs‚Äù who release their own version of it, so I want my version to remain the gold standard that people will return to again and again.

My primary income source for the last twenty years has been playing poker professionally. I love gambling, I love strategy games and I really love gambling games in which it is possible to be profitable in the long term when you apply the proper strategies.
